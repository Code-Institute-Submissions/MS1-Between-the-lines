About

More than a simple website, Between the Lines is a community of readers who don't want their hobby to be old-school anymore. Rejoin us and learn, discover, laugh and discuss freely with people sharing the same interests as you.

Every month, our team of experts spend hours reading critics, forums, old and new-born books in order to select the ideal one for you and your community while getting the best from our meeting together.

All meetings are free and online - so as you can join us from wherever you are located in the world.

Now : chose a topic you have an interest in - and let yourself go into this new adventure!
